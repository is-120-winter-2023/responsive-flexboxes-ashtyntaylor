Chicken Pot Pie

Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C). In a large skillet, sauté the onion and celery in butter until tender. Stir in the flour, salt, pepper, and celery seed until well blended. Gradually stir in the chicken broth and milk, and bring to a boil. Cook and stir for 2 minutes or until thickened. Stir in the chicken and mixed vegetables. Remove from heat. Place one pie crust in a 9-inch pie plate. Pour the chicken mixture into the crust. Top with the second crust. Cut slits in the top to allow steam to escape. Bake for 30 to 35 minutes or until crust is golden brown.

Chocolate Chip Cookies

Beat the butter and sugars, then beat in the eggs and vanilla. Dissolve the baking soda in hot water and add to the mixture. Stir in the flour, chocolate chips, and walnuts. Drop dough onto a prepared baking sheet. Bake until the edges are golden brown.
